Donations From Families, Staff, Anyone

To make our auction successful, we need donations from the extended community and you.
Donations are due Feb 16 so we can process and prepare them, but we can take late entries up to March 9.

Do You Have a Donation?

Here's what to do if you've gotten a donation from a business, or want to donate something yourself:

Tell us about it... THEN bring gift cards/certificates to the office, marked with your name and the value of the card/certificate OR email electronic certificates to pta-auction@lakeshoreelementary.org OR email pta-auction@lakeshoreelementary.org to arrange a handoff in person.

How Can You Ask for a Donation?

Want to ask a business for a donation but don't know how? Check out our solicitation page for tips.

Donation Ideas

Lakeshore Gatherings

Anyone can sponsor a group event like bike riding, hiking, baking a cake, a magic show, movie outing, or LEGO party.

If you have access to a cool workplace like an animation studio or museum, offer a private tour.

Do you have VIP status anywhere that entitles you to deals? Use it to sponsor an activity at a discounted rate unavailable to the public. Be creative!

Ideas from last year's auction:

  • A two-hour Visual Art workshop for six kids, grades K-5, led by Lakeshore parent and artist Ranu Mukherjee
  • A private planetarium show at SFSU for a small group with Lakeshore parent and astronomer Dr. Chris McCarthy
  • Kids Hike, Play and Picnic in the Presidio hosted by Lakeshore parent Laura Batie
  • Private pool and BBQ party for up to 50 guests hosted by Lakeshore parent Lizzie Liu
  • Two rounds of golf for two at the Olympic Club, sponsored by Lakeshore family, the Doyles

Services

Everyone has a talent they can put to use for Lakeshore. Volunteer your skills for a good cause!

Donate babysitting, house cleaning, cooking a 3-course meal, home organization, yard work, tutoring, even pie-of-the-month! Teach someone how to sew or build a web page. The possibilities are endless.

Perfect if you prefer to donate time and expertise instead of cash.

Vacation Properties

Do you share a cabin that stays empty most of the year? Donate a weekend or two to the auction.

For instance, two years ago, we had two nights at a condo in North Lake Tahoe (sleeps five) donated by Ms. Sadja.

Gift Cards and Goods

That gift card you haven't used in three years isn't doing you any good. Drop it in the PTA inbox and let it raise money for the school.

If you receive a gift over the holidays that just isn't "you," clear out that clutter! We'll take new items such as books, appliances, and unused clothes.

Beer and Wine

A popular auction item, wine gift baskets can be made from your donations of wine. Looking to help but short on time? Scoop up a nice wine (value $20 or more) the next time you see a sale, and let us auction it for its face value for a good cause.

Contact pta-auction@LakeshoreElementary.org to donate wine off school premises.
