Volunteer at and Before the Auction

We need a great crew to help with preproduction and venue setup/cleanup.

Preproduction includes creating brochures, packaging donations, and building out our online catalog.

Day-of help includes:

  • Setting up
  • Decorating
  • Laying out donations
  • Serving alcohol – we would love individuals with bartenders' licenses
  • Cleaning up

Your help makes a huge impact!

If you can assist, please sign up or email pta-auction@LakeshoreElementary.org.

Thank You!
