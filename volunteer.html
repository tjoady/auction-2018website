Volunteer At and Before the Auction

We need a great crew to help with preproduction and venue setup, cleanup, and operations. Sign up to help today.

Preproduction includes:

  • Creating a slideshow
  • Creating programs and signage
  • Pricing out wine donations
  • Packaging donations
  • Building out our online catalog
  • Picking up beer
  • Pickup up equipment
  • Shopping for ingredients

Day-of help includes:

  • Setting up
  • Decorating
  • Laying out donations
  • Checking guests in and out
  • Selling mini-raffle tickets
  • Recording live auction bidders
  • Entering winning bid numbers
  • Handing out certificates and items to winning bidders
  • Photographer/videographer
  • Cleaning up

We need a big team to pull this off. Your help makes a huge impact!

If you can assist, please sign up online at Signupgenius or email pta-auction@LakeshoreElementary.org.

Thank You!
